A Syriac Fragment from The Cause of All Causes on the Pillars of Hercules

Adam C. McCollum

Abstract: This brief note draws attention to a passage from the Syriac Cause of All Causes that describes the Pillars of Hercules, but as being three in number rather than two. The Syriac text in question has been well-known since it was published in 1889, but this particular passage is studied and commented on here especially from a recently cataloged manuscript from Dayr Al-Za‘farān, in which the passage is completely divorced from its context in the Cause of All Causes.
